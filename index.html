Howdy!

My name is Alaina Vasquez. I live out my dream as a wife, mother, and pet-parent, dedicated to encouraging a zest for life, prompting smiles, and inspiring a passion for learning with everyone I encounter. I thrive with my family in Dallas, Texas, and in my free time, I enjoy reading/writing, watching movies, playing board/card games, and sharing groan-worthy puns. My inspiration is sourced from the greatest Author of them all, Jesus Christ, and I strive to show my love through obedience to His Word. (Hebrews 12:2)
As a manager with an extensive background in IT, I love making the stakeholder journey a wonderful experience, making clarity out of ambiguity, solving puzzles, and discovering new aspects of the industry. I excel at bringing people together and enjoy contributing to mission-driven businesses, providing a positive impact in the world through my work.
